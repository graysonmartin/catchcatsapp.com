how catching works

three taps. that's the whole thing.

🐈
step one

spot one

your cat. a café cat. a park cat. if you see it, you can catch it.

»
📸
step two

snap + caption

photo, a lil caption, and on-device AI guesses the breed. tabby, maine coon, or a classic domestic shorthair.

»
🗺️
step three

keep it forever

it's pinned to your map and added to your collection. 

breed brain

it knows the breed

point, snap, done. the breed-ID runs entirely on your phone. no external AI. works fully offline, even with zero bars in some alley. let's see if you caught a tabby, maine coon, sphynx, or something else.

your cat map

every cat, pinned

a worldwide map of everywhere you've met a cat. that alley in lisbon, the café in tokyo, your nonna's porch. watch the pins pile up.

your collection

gotta meet 'em all

every breed you discover fills in a tile. the "?" ones are still out there somewhere, waiting to be met. each cat keeps its full encounter history, too.

the feed

a tiny social network. only for cats.

follow your friends, like n comment, reply in threads, @mention each other. double-tap to like. doomscroll cats all day :3
